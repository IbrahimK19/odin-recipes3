Sandwich recipe

Description

A sandwich is a popular, portable dish consisting of fillings—typically meat, cheese, or vegetables—placed between two or more slices of bread, or inside a split roll. It can also be an open-faced dish with a single slice of bread topped with ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Bread: 2-3 slices
  • Butter: 2-3 tsp
  • Vegetables: Thinly sliced cucumber, onion, tomato, and bell peppers

Steps

  1. Slice all vegetables thinly.
  2. Spread butter on one side of each bread slice.
  3. Sprinkle with salt, pepper, or masala, then place a slice of cheese on top.
  4. Top with the second slice of bread (buttered side down)
