Cold Coffee

Description

Cold Coffee is a popular chilled beverage made with coffee, sugar and milk/water. It is made in so many different ways across the world with varying ingredients like whipping cream, ice cream, coffee syrup etc.

ingredients

  • instant coffee
  • milk
  • sugar
  • whipping cream

Steps

  1. : In a blender, combine 2 tablespoons of instant coffee granules, 2 tablespoons of sugar (or to taste), and 2 tablespoons of warm water.
  2. Blend until the mixture becomes light, frothy, and creamy.
  3. Add 1 cup of chilled full-fat milk and 4–6 ice cubes (or frozen milk cubes for extra creaminess) to the blender
  4. Blend again until the mixture is frothy and smooth
